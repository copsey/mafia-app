=Help: Dealer=

*Alignment:* Mafia.
*Ability:* Peddle drugs.
*To win:* Survive.

*Description:* The Dealer is part of the mafia. Once each night, the Dealer can target another player, making that player appear as suspicious if investigated during the night.

*Notes:*
- The Dealer's ability has no effect on roles that already appear as suspicious.
- The effect of the Dealer's ability wears off at the end of the night.
